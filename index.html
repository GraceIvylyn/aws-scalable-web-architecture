Waakye with gari & shito

A plate of waakye reminds us that simple ingredients can create unforgettable greatness.

Waakye is one of Ghana's most loved meals, usually served with rice, beans, gari, spaghetti, egg, meat, salad, and spicy shito.

Bio

Meet Chef Ama Nkrumah.

Chef Ama Nkrumah is a Ghanaian food creator, chef, and African cuisine advocate. She is passionate about showing the beauty of local meals and helping people appreciate the history, flavour, and identity behind African food.

Through cooking classes, food stories, digital recipes, and community events, she promotes dishes such as waakye, banku, jollof, kontomire stew, fufu, kelewele, tuo zaafi, and other local meals loved across Ghana and West Africa.

Her mission is simple: make African food respected, enjoyed, and celebrated everywhere.
